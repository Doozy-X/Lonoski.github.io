About us.

Our company was founded in 1996 by Milos Ognjanovski, at the beginning as an industrial design company, later growing into a full production company. Today we make ideas into products, we are completely dedicated to our clients ideas and requests and are very flexible towards their needs. Our quality is based on the history of our company, alongside the trust of the current, and past clients satisfaction. Our primary focus is plastic production also specializing in complex molds for blow molding, thermoforming, plastic injection molding and aluminum injection molding. Recently we decided to enter a new field, challenging the new demands of the industry for automation of the industrial processes such as packaging lines, assembling lines, etc.
